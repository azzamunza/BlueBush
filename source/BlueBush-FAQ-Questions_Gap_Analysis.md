# BlueBush Frequently Asked Questions (Development List)

This document outlines potential customer inquiries. We will use this list to identify data gaps in our product catalog.

## 1. General Brand & Ethics
* Where are your products made?
* Is your packaging plastic-free?
* Do you offer a warranty on your products?
* Are your products vegan?
* Do you test on animals?
* Is your timber FSC certified?
* Do you offer trade discounts for businesses?
* Can I pick up my order from your Perth warehouse?

## 2. Textiles (Linen, Bamboo, Hemp)
* **Linen:**
    * What is the GSM (weight) of your linen?
    * Does the linen shrink in the wash?
    * Why is my linen shedding fluff?
    * Do I need to iron the linen sheets?
    * Is the linen French or Belgian flax?
* **Bamboo:**
    * Is your bamboo fabric Lyocell or Viscose?
    * What is the thread count of the bamboo sheets?
    * Can I put bamboo sheets in the dryer?
    * Is the bamboo grown without pesticides?
* **Weighted Blankets:**
    * How do I wash the weighted blanket?
    * Is the weight from plastic pellets or glass beads?
    * What weight should I choose for my body size?

## 3. Kitchen & Dining (Ceramics, Wood, Silicone)
* **Ceramics:**
    * Are the ceramics dishwasher safe?
    * Can I put the ramen bowls in the microwave?
    * Is the glaze lead-free and non-toxic?
    * Do the plates scratch easily with metal cutlery?
* **Wood:**
    * How do I treat the acacia serving paddle?
    * Can I put wooden items in the dishwasher?
* **Silicone & Wraps:**
    * Are the silicone lids BPA free?
    * Can I use beeswax wraps on raw meat?
    * How long do beeswax wraps last?
    * How do I revive my beeswax wraps if they lose stickiness?
    * Are the silicone lids oven safe?

## 4. Beauty & Personal Care
* **Ingredients & Safety:**
    * Are your products safe to use during pregnancy?
    * Do your products contain palm oil?
    * Are your products gluten-free? (Critical for lip balms/toothpastes)
    * Do you use synthetic fragrances or just essential oils?
    * Is the packaging compostable or recyclable?
* **Usage:**
    * Does the natural deodorant contain bicarbonate soda? (Causes irritation for some)
    * How many washes does a shampoo bar last?
    * Will the tinted lip balm stain my clothes?
    * Is the sunscreen suitable for sensitive skin/eczema?
    * Does the sunscreen leave a white cast?

## 5. Cleaning & Laundry
* **Safety:**
    * Are your cleaning products septic tank safe?
    * Is the laundry detergent grey-water safe for my garden?
    * Are the cleaning bombs safe for pets?
* **Usage:**
    * Do the laundry sheets work in cold water?
    * Can I use the stain remover on wool/silk?
    * Are the wool dryer balls scented?

## 6. Wellness Tech & Garden
* **Tech:**
    * Is the diffuser corded or battery operated?
    * Does the diffuser turn off automatically when empty?
    * How loud is the air purifier?
    * How often do I need to change the HEPA filter?
    * How long does the solar power bank take to charge in the sun?
* **Garden:**
    * Are the seeds suitable for planting in [Specific Season]?
    * Will the native seeds grow in humid climates (like Queensland)?
    * Does the bee hotel need to be cleaned?

## 7. Returns & Shipping (Logistics)
* How much is shipping to [Location]?
* Do you ship internationally?
* What is your return policy on sale items?
* Can I return opened beauty products if I react to them?
* How long does dispatch take?
