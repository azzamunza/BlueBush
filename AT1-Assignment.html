Section 3
AI Automation Opportunities — Identification
✓ Complete

From the processes you identified in Section 2, select at least 3 processes that could benefit from AI automation. For each opportunity:

  • Name the process
  • Explain WHY this process is suitable for AI automation (repetitive? data-heavy? pattern recognition?)
  • Describe what type of AI tool could be applied (e.g., chatbot, recommendation engine, predictive analytics, automated email, image recognition)

Tip: Consider the VETASSESS criteria for AI suitability: repetitive tasks, large data volumes, pattern recognition, natural language processing needs.

Section 4

✓ Complete

  • Risk: What are the potential risks or downsides?

Tip: A simple scoring table works well here. Consider using the Quill editor's table feature.

Section 5
Recommended AI Solution — Detail
✓ Complete

Section 6
Implementation Plan
✓ Complete

Create a high-level implementation plan for your recommended AI solution. Include:

  • Phase 1 – Planning (Week 1–2): What needs to be done to prepare?

Section 7
Risk Assessment
✓ Complete

Identify and assess at least 4 risks associated with implementing your recommended AI solution at BlueBush. For each risk:

Consider risks such as: data privacy, customer trust/acceptance, staff training needs, technology failures, cost overruns, incorrect AI outputs.
